    DebConf is a free event that offers Debian developers a place to meet, share ideas and collaborate.

    DebConf15 will take place in Heidelberg, Germany, Europe, Earth.

  • Volunteering

    DebConf needs you! Every year, the DebConf team is assisted by an enthusiastic team of volunteers to help out with various tasks and make the event a fun and safe place for all our visitors.

  • Mailing List

    You can subscribe to receive updates from the debconf-announce (general announces), debconf-team (organization stuffs) and debconf-video (video-team stuffs) in DebConf mailing lists

    IRC is available in #debconf-team and #debconf-video (#debConf-team webchat, #debconf-video webchat) on OFTC
